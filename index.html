Geo-Based Social Network

What is more important to you?

Connect and know about stuff that happens far far away from you, or stuff that is happening right now on your location?

EARSTUFF offers the oportunity to connect to the world that is around you, in a better way!

The social part of the network presents posts from friends and public posts from people who are close to us. There is still the possibility of pulling someone else's post to our location, if it is interesting. The objective is to create a social filter in which posts near you, posts from friends, or interesting posts for someone close to you will be displayed.

As basic functions, the network has the ability to share information, either in posts or through private chat between users. It also has customizable personal pages and a follower system.

The social network will also have a part to integrate and connect standard users to the businesses they like so much. The aim is to implement mechanisms and tools to facilitate connections between users and improve the ecosystem we live in.

This part is still under study and development.

Culture is important to all of us, since prehistoric times. And as such, the event component on a social network could not be neglected. Once again, the main objective is to facilitate contact and communication, through social tools.

These tools are still being planned, to better meet users' expectations and needs.
